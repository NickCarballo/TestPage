About me!





My name is Nicholas Carballo and I am a Senior student studying Information Technology! I currently attend Florida International University but will be graduating December 13, 2019! I have a strong passion for technology and have been taking computers apart since I was a kid. My main focus in technology is building a bridge between technology and the user. The idea of a person not being able to enjoy a technology solely due to a poor user interface is a true tragedy. I love technologies and want as many people to enjoy them as possible. We went from only scientist using computers to most people having a computer in their pocket. I want to contribute to the usability of existing and upcoming technologies.



Apart from my school's curriculum I have been studying front end development, using coding languages such as html, css and javascript. Other hobbies of mine include BMX, Photography, Videography and photo/video editing. I am familiar with Adobe Lightroom and Adobe Photoshop. I am also familiar with several video editing softwares such as Adobe premiere elements, Adobe Premiere Pro, Adobe After Effects and Sony Vegas Pro.
